Keyboard  and mouse controls
A & left arrow - move left

D & right arrow - move right

W & up arrow - jump

Space & z - melee attack
LMB & x - ranged attack 1
RMB & c - ranged attack 2
k - kill

Controller controls
Left stick & dpad - move left & right
A - jump
X - melee attack
B - ranged attack 1
Y - ranged attack 2
